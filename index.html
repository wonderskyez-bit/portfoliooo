About 01
I'm a rising senior at Princeton studying Psychology with minors in French Language and Values & Public Life. I'm on a pre-law track, interested in using my psychology background to explore how law intersects with human behavior, identity, and narrative. Outside the classroom, I research, code, write, direct, and build — sometimes all at once.

In my free time, I like to think creatively about everyday problems — teaching myself whatever it takes, from new tools to new code, to build solutions to them. My work in film has been shown at the Venice and Mallorca Art Biennales, and on campus I put my skills to work across research labs, media teams, and student communities.
